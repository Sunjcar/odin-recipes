Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Spicy Korean style fried rice with kimchi and spam. It is a widely popular korean dish in the States!

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Day Old Rice
  • Spam
  • 1 Egg
  • Diced Scallions
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Heat up vegetable oil in a sauce pan until a light smoke is visible.
  2. Add spam on to the sauce pan and sear until both sides are golden brown 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add in rice and and kimchi and stir fry all ingredients together for 5 minutes
  4. While rice is cooking take a small skillet and cook eggs on the side how ever style you want
  5. Take your finished egg and add it into your sauce pan with the other ingredients
  6. Garnish with diced scallions
